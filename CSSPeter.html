Peter Griffin, a TV Icon

Peter Griffin is the lovable, goofy dad of the Griffin family in Family Guy, known for his big heart and even bigger sense of humor.

Peter's Antics

Peter's antics often involve wild schemes and outlandish adventures, which always add a hilarious twist to life in the Griffin household.

Peter's Humor

Peter's humor is bold and goofy, often catching people off guard with its randomness, but it's his carefree attitude that makes it funny.
